FAQ

What to know before you run the checker

Is my data uploaded?

No. The checker runs in your browser and does not send your entries to a server.

What does the score measure?

It checks product clarity, answer coverage, buyer-intent context, and signals such as FAQs, comparison content, llms.txt, and Schema.

Does a high score guarantee AI citations or rankings?

No. The score is a readiness guide, not a prediction or guarantee.

What is included in the free checker?

A score, missing signals, priority fixes, FAQ ideas, an llms.txt draft, and a Schema JSON-LD draft.

What is the difference between Fix Kit and Pro Kit?

Fix Kit covers common page and asset gaps. Pro Kit adds planning, tracking, testing, and a broader site-wide workflow.

What happens in the Mini Audit?

A human reviews your homepage and up to two important pages, then delivers prioritized issues and a concise action plan.
